About Us

Coastal Mountain Handyman brings over 15 years of hands-on experience in residential home repair and improvement. Our owner, a certified handyperson with deep roots in the coastal mountain community, founded this business on a simple principle: deliver exceptional craftsmanship with unmatched customer service.

We understand that your home is your most valuable investment. That's why we treat every project—big or small—with the same level of attention and care. Our team stays current with the latest techniques and best practices in the handyman industry, ensuring that your repairs are not just fixed, but fixed right.

From emergency repairs to planned renovations, we're committed to being your trusted partner for all your home maintenance needs. When you call Coastal Mountain Handyman, you're not just hiring a service—you're partnering with dedicated professionals who genuinely care about your home.
