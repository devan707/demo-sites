About John Rugani Roofing

With over 30 years of experience in the roofing industry, John Rugani has built a reputation for excellence, integrity, and customer-first service in Santa Cruz and surrounding areas. What started as a passion for quality craftsmanship has grown into a trusted roofing company serving hundreds of satisfied residential and commercial clients. John and his skilled team approach every project with meticulous attention to detail, using premium materials and proven techniques to deliver roofs that protect and beautify properties for decades. We believe in transparent communication, competitive pricing, and building lasting relationships with our clients. Whether it's a simple repair or a complete replacement, we treat every job like it's our own home.
