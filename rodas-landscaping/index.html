About Rodas Landscaping

For over 20 years, Rodas Landscaping has been Santa Cruz's trusted name in professional landscaping and lawn care. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the most respected landscaping companies in the region, serving hundreds of satisfied residential and commercial clients.

Our success is built on three core principles: exceptional craftsmanship, reliable service, and customer satisfaction. Every team member is trained to the highest standards and takes pride in transforming outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional environments. Whether you need routine maintenance or a complete landscape redesign, we bring the same dedication and expertise to every project.

We're proud to be part of the Santa Cruz community and committed to sustainable, eco-friendly landscaping practices that enhance both your property and our environment.
