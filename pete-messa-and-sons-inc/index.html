About Pete Messa & Sons Inc

Pete Messa & Sons Inc has been serving the Los Gatos community for over three decades with unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Founded by Pete Messa, a visionary in the excavating industry, our company has grown to become a trusted partner for both residential and commercial property owners throughout the region.

Our team combines extensive field experience with cutting-edge equipment and modern techniques. Every project is approached with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that whether it's a simple grading job or a complex excavation project, your property receives the professional care it deserves. We take pride in our reputation for reliability, competitive pricing, and exceptional workmanship that consistently exceeds expectations.
