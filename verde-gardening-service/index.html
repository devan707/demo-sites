About Verde Gardening Service

Verde Gardening Service is San Francisco's premier gardening and landscaping provider, led by owner Sylvio. With a deep passion for horticulture and an unwavering commitment to excellence, Sylvio has built a reputation for transforming outdoor spaces into stunning natural environments that clients absolutely love.

Our team brings together years of experience in landscape design, plant care, and sustainable gardening practices. We take pride in understanding each client's unique vision and delivering results that exceed expectations. Whether you're looking for regular maintenance, a complete garden redesign, or specialized services, Verde Gardening has the expertise and dedication to make your outdoor space truly special.

We believe that a beautiful garden enhances not just your property, but your quality of life. That's why we approach every project with meticulous attention to detail, environmental responsibility, and genuine care for the long-term health and beauty of your outdoor environment.
