About Marcelo Ruiz

With over 10 years of dedicated experience in handyman services and landscaping, Marcelo Ruiz has built a reputation for excellence and reliability in Santa Cruz and surrounding communities. Starting as an apprentice with a passion for craftsmanship, Marcelo has grown to lead a team that tackles everything from minor home repairs to complete landscape transformations.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. We believe that every home deserves exceptional service, and every customer deserves clear communication and honest pricing. Whether you're dealing with a leaky faucet, planning a deck renovation, or dreaming of your ideal landscape, we bring the same level of professionalism and care to every project.

Our 4.8-star rating and numerous satisfied customers are a testament to the trust we've built in this community. We take pride in showing up on time, respecting your home, and delivering results that exceed expectations. When you choose Marcelo Ruiz Handyman & Landscaping Service, you're choosing a partner dedicated to making your home beautiful and functional.
