About Castro Construction

Castro Construction Inc. has been serving the Freedom community and surrounding areas for over two decades, building a reputation for excellence, integrity, and uncompromising quality. Our experienced team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver exceptional results on every project, from intimate home renovations to comprehensive new construction. We believe that great construction is about more than just building—it's about understanding our clients' needs, respecting their homes, and creating spaces that truly enhance their lives. Whether you're planning a kitchen remodel, a bathroom update, or a complete home renovation, you can trust Castro Construction to bring your vision to reality with the professionalism and attention to detail you deserve.
